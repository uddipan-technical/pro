Who We Are 
 
We are based in Windsor. Our friendly team of inventory experts all come from a property background having been landlords, investors and former tenants. With changes made by the government in 2007, we realised early on there was clearly a need for independent inventory professionals. Hence we identified a gap in the market for premium Inventory services. 
 
Over the years we have seen the many costly problems that frequently arise from inaccurate and badly detailed inventory reports and we can help to solve these problems.
